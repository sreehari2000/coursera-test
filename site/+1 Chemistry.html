S-block Elements

Elements that are having electronic configuration ns1-2, in which last electrons are entering into s-orbit are called s-block elements. elements of group-1 and group-2 are s-block elements

Group-1/Alkali metals

Group-1 consist of Li, Na, K, Rb, Cs, Fr are called alkali metals because they form hydroxides on reaction with water that are strongly alkaline. Amonf alkali metals Na and K are abundant. Fr is radioactive, with a t1/2 of 21 minutes.
